About Hope Foundation

A community-first nonprofit working with schools, shelters, and local leaders to help every child learn with dignity, stay healthy, and dream bigger.

Who we are

We are educators, social workers, and volunteers who co-create programs with the communities we serve. Our teams run learning centers, nutrition drives, and health camps across urban and rural clusters.

Every rupee is tracked against clear outcomes—attendance, learning levels, nutrition status, and family engagement—so supporters see real progress.

What guides us

  • Community-led design and women-led facilitation wherever possible.
  • Transparent funds with dashboards and audited reports.
  • Child safety, dignity, and inclusive classrooms for all abilities.
  • Impact measured with baselines, follow-ups, and partner reviews.

What we do each week

  • Early learning corners for ages 3–10 with story-led teaching.
  • After-school support, STEM clubs, and board-exam prep for teens.
  • Nutritious meals, health check-ups, and menstrual health sessions.
  • Life-skills, career mentoring, and digital literacy labs.
  • Parent circles that turn caregivers into co-educators.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate?

You can donate online through our secure payment gateway (UPI/Card), or by bank transfer. All donations are tax-deductible under 80G.

How do I become a volunteer?

Visit our Volunteer page and fill out the application form. We'll contact you within 48 hours to discuss opportunities.

Do you accept corporate partnerships?

Yes! We welcome CSR partnerships. Email us at partnerships@hopefoundation.org to discuss collaboration opportunities.

Can I get donation receipts?

Absolutely! All donors receive an 80G tax-exemption receipt via email within 24 hours of their donation.
